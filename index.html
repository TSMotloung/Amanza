LEARN MORE

Store Smartly

To keep your perfume at its best, store it in a cool, dry place like the back of a drawer or a cupboard away from direct sunlight.

Fridge Trick

For an extra touch of refinement, place it in the fridge for two days before first use. This helps stabilize the blend and enhances its longevity.

Moisturize First

Apply perfume to well hydrated skin or use an unscented lotion to help lock in the fragrance.

Pulse Points Matter

Spritz perfume on pulse points like the wrists, neck, behind the ears, and inner elbows for better diffusion.

Don't Rub It In

Rubbing your wrists together breaks down fragrance molecules and makes the scent fade faster. Let it dry naturally

Layer for Longevity

Use a matching scented body lotion or oil before applying perfume to enhance and prolong the scent.

Refresh with Hair & Clothes

Lightly mist your hairbrush or clothing (from a distance) for a subtle, long lasting scent.
